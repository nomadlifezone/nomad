# China Shared Services Complete Guide

China pioneered the shared economy with services like shared bikes, power banks, and more. This guide shows you how to use these convenient services as a foreigner.

## 🚲 Shared Bikes (共享单车)

### Major Providers

| Provider | App | Price | Features | Availability |
|----------|-----|-------|----------|--------------|
| **Meituan Bike** (美团单车) | Meituan App | ¥1.5-2/30min | Most common ⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐ | Nationwide |
| **Qingju** (青桔单车) | Qingju App | ¥1.5-2/30min | Clean bikes ⭐⭐⭐⭐ | Major cities |
| **Hello Bike** (哈啰单车) | Hello Bike | ¥1.5-2/30min | Good coverage ⭐⭐⭐⭐ | Wide coverage |
| **Didi Bike** (滴滴单车) | DiDi App | ¥1.5-2/30min | Integrated with DiDi ⭐⭐⭐ | Some cities |

### How to Use

**Step 1: Download App and Register**

1. **Meituan Bike** (Recommended):
   - Download Meituan App
   - Sign up with Chinese phone number
   - Or use WeChat mini-program

2. **Qingju**:
   - Download Qingju App
   - Register with phone number
   - Use Alipay for deposit

3. **Hello Bike**:
   - Download Hello Bike App
   - Register with passport possible
   - Alipay deposit

**Step 2: Deposit (押金)**

- Most require ¥50-299 deposit
- Refundable when you stop using
- Can use credit to skip deposit

**Step 3: Find and Scan**

1. **Find a bike**:
   - Look for bikes with QR codes on fender or handlebar
   - Check bike condition before scanning

2. **Scan QR code**:
   - Open app → Scan → Camera on bike
   - Wait for unlock sound/beep

3. **Start riding**:
   - Check brakes and bell
   - Adjust seat height
   - Lock bike properly when done

### Pricing

| Provider | Price per 30 min | Daily Cap | Monthly |
|----------|-------------------|----------|---------|
| Meituan | ¥1.5-2 | ¥5-10 | ¥15-35 |
| Qingju | ¥1.5-2 | ¥5-10 | ¥15-35 |
| Hello | ¥1.5-2 | ¥5-10 | ¥15-35 |

**Payment**: Alipay, WeChat Pay, or in-app balance

### Parking Rules

- **Must park in designated areas** (停车点)
- Look for white paint marks or virtual zones
- **Violation fees**: ¥5-20 if parked incorrectly
- Some areas prohibited ( metro stations, hospitals)

### Tips

- ✅ Check brakes before riding
- ✅ Adjust seat to proper height
- ✅ Follow traffic rules
- ✅ Park at bike stations to avoid fines
- ❌ Don't park blocking walkways
- ❌ Don't ride on sidewalks where prohibited

## 🔋 Shared Power Banks (共享充电宝)

### Major Providers

| Provider | Price | Features | Locations |
|----------|-------|----------|----------|
| **Monster** (怪兽充电) | ¥3-5/30min | Widely available ⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐ | Restaurants, malls |
| **Jumei** (聚美) | ¥3-5/30min | Popular ⭐⭐⭐⭐ | Shopping areas |
| **Meituan Charging** | ¥3-5/30min | Integrated app ⭐⭐⭐⭐ | Various |
| **Laite** (来电) | ¥3-5/30min | Good network ⭐⭐⭐⭐ | Metro, malls |

### How to Use

**Step 1: Locate a Power Bank Station**

Common locations:
- Restaurants and cafes
- Shopping malls
- Metro stations
- Hotels
- KTV and bars
- Convenience stores

**Step 2: Rent Power Bank**

**Via WeChat/Alipay**:
1. Open WeChat → Scan QR code on station
2. Or use specific app
3. Authenticate with phone number
4. Pay deposit (¥0-50) or use credit

**Step 3: Take and Use**

1. Power bank ejects from slot
2. Take the small end (USB-C/Lightning/Micro-USB)
3. Plug into your phone
4. Charges while you walk around

**Step 4: Return**

1. Find any station of the same brand
2. Insert power bank into any empty slot
3. Wait for confirmation beep
4. Check app that rental ended

### Pricing

| Duration | Cost |
|----------|------|
| First 5 minutes | Often free |
| Per 30 minutes | ¥3-5 |
| Daily maximum | ¥20-30 |
| Lost power bank | ¥99-299 |

**Note**: If you don't return within 24 hours, considered purchase.

### Tips

- ✅ Return to same brand stations
- ✅ Keep phone in pocket/bag while charging
- ✅ Return before leaving venue
- ❌ Don't forget to return (expensive!)
- ❌ Check return slot is empty before inserting

### Troubleshooting

**Bike won't unlock**:
- Check network connection
- Try another bike
- Restart app

**Power bank won't charge**:
- Try different cable type
- Check if power bank has charge
- Return and rent another

**Can't find bikes nearby**:
- Check app for bike locations
- Walk to area with more bikes
- Try different provider
