# Complete Xi'an Historical and Cultural Travel Guide

Xi'an, capital of 13 dynasties, an important cradle of Chinese civilization. From the world-shaking Terracotta Warriors to the magnificent Ancient City Wall, this guide takes you through thousands of years of history.

## 🗺️ Must-Visit Attractions

### 1. Terracotta Warriors Museum ⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐

**Admission**: ¥120 (advance online booking required)
**Hours**: 8:30-18:00
**Duration**: 3-4 hours

**Three Pits**:
- **Pit 1**: Largest and most spectacular, 6,000+ terracotta warriors
- **Pit 2**: Premium exhibits, close-up viewing
- **Pit 3**: Command headquarters, smallest scale

**Tour Route**:
1. Visit order: Pit 1 → Pit 3 → Pit 2
2. Watch documentary first (understand background)
3. Rent audio guide (¥30) or hire guide (¥200-300)
4. Focus on Pit 1 panorama and Bronze Chariot Hall

**Ticket Booking**: 1-7 days in advance online, real-name required

**Transportation**:
- City bus Route 5 (East Square of Railway Station)
- Journey: 1.5 hours, ¥7
- Or chartered car/tour group

### 2. Xi'an City Wall ⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐

**Admission**: ¥54 (South Gate)
**Hours**: 8:00-22:00 (extended in summer)
**Duration**: 2-3 hours

**Activities**:
- **Walking**: Free, full circuit 14 km (3-4 hours)
- **Cycling**: ¥45/2 hours (recommended)
- **Tourist cart**: ¥80/circuit

**Best Experience**:
- 🚴 Rent bicycle for full circuit (recommended)
- 🌅 Sunset time on wall (17:00-19:00)
- 🌃 Evening wall night view (light show)

**Wall Entrances**:
- South Gate (Yongning Gate) - Most recommended, most magnificent
- East, West, North Gates also available

### 3. Big Wild Goose Pagoda + Tang Paradise ⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐

**Big Wild Goose Pagoda**:
- Admission: ¥40 (climb ¥30 extra)
- Where Xuanzang stored scriptures after returning from India
- Duration: 1 hour

**North Square Fountain**:
- Free music fountain (Asia's largest)
- Time: 12:00, 20:30 (summer), 21:00 (winter)
- Closed Mondays for maintenance (except holidays)

**Tang Paradise Street** ⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐:
- Free!
- Most beautiful at night (brilliant lights)
- Tang culture themed street
- Various performances and internet celebrity check-in spots
- "Never Falling Lady" performance (super popular)
- Duration: 2-3 hours

### 4. Muslim Quarter (Huifang) ⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐

**Location**: Near Drum Tower
**Features**: Muslim community, food paradise

**Must-Try Food** (see Food section)

**Visit Tips**:
- More lively at night
- Very crowded during peak times
- Explore side alleys for authentic local shops

**Nearby Attractions**:
- Drum Tower (¥35)
- Bell Tower (¥35)
- Combined ticket (¥50)

### 5. Shaanxi History Museum ⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐

**Admission**: Free (reservation required) + Special exhibitions ¥30
**Hours**: 9:00-17:30 (Closed Mondays)
**Duration**: 2-3 hours

**Reservation**: 7 days in advance online, daily limit 6,000 people

**Visit Tips**:
- Hire guide or rent audio guide
- Highlights: Tang Dynasty murals, bronzeware
- Crowded, arrive at 9 AM recommended

### 6. Huaqing Pool + Mount Li ⭐⭐⭐⭐

**Admission**: ¥120 (includes Mount Li)
**Duration**: 3-4 hours

**Highlights**:
- Tang Dynasty royal hot spring
- Where Yang Guifei bathed
- Xi'an Incident site
- Mount Li scenery

**"Song of Everlasting Sorrow" Show** ⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐:
- Large outdoor performance
- Love story of Emperor Xuanzong and Yang Guifei
- Tickets: ¥238-988
- Time: 20:30 start (April-October)
- Highly recommended!

**Transportation**: 30 km from city, Route 5 bus (same as Terracotta Warriors)

## 🍜 Xi'an Cuisine

### Must-Try Food TOP 10

**1. Roujiamo (Chinese Burger)** ⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐
- Recommended: Fanji, Qinyu
- Price: ¥15-20

**2. Yangrou Paomo (Lamb Stew with Bread)** ⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐
- Xi'an's signature dish
- Recommended: Lao Sun Jia, Lao Mi Jia
- Price: ¥35-50
- How to eat: Break bread yourself (smaller is better)

**3. Liangpi (Cold Noodles)** ⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐
- Summer must-eat
- Types: Rice skin, flour skin
- Price: ¥8-12

**4. Hu La Tang (Spicy Soup)** ⭐⭐⭐⭐
- Breakfast staple
- Pair with fried bread or roujiamo
- Price: ¥8-12

**5. Biang Biang Noodles** ⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐
- Shaanxi's widest noodles
- "Biang" is most complex Chinese character
- Price: ¥15-25

**6. Hulutou Paomo** ⭐⭐⭐⭐
- Pork intestine stew with bread
- Recommended: Chunfasheng
- Price: ¥30-40

**7. Soup Dumplings** ⭐⭐⭐⭐
- Recommended: Jia San Soup Dumplings
- Price: ¥20-30/steamer

**8. Sour Soup Dumplings** ⭐⭐⭐⭐
- Specialty dumplings
- Sour-spicy flavor
- Price: ¥15-25

**9. Grilled Meat** ⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐
- Muslim Quarter specialty
- Lamb skewers
- Price: ¥3-5/skewer

**10. Persimmon Cake** ⭐⭐⭐⭐
- Autumn specialty snack
- Price: ¥5-8/piece

## ⚠️ Important Notes

### Best Travel Time
- **Spring (Mar-May)**: Pleasant climate ⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐
- **Autumn (Sep-Nov)**: Clear and comfortable, best ⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐
- **Summer (Jun-Aug)**: Hot, but rich nightlife ⭐⭐⭐
- **Winter (Dec-Feb)**: Cold, but fewer tourists ⭐⭐⭐

### Practical Tips
1. **Terracotta Warriors tickets must book in advance**
2. **Shaanxi Museum needs 7-day advance grab**
3. Xi'an noodle portions large, women can order small portions
4. Muslim Quarter prices inflated, can go to Sajinqiao
5. City Wall cycling: watch safety, control speed
6. Bring stomach medicine (food heavy-flavored)
7. Download Xi'an Metro App and Dianping

### Anti-Scam Guide
- ❌ Don't take black cars near railway station
- ❌ Don't trust "Terracotta Warriors shuttle bus" touts
- ❌ Route 5 only at East Square of Railway Station, others are fake
- ❌ Muslim Quarter prices high, rational consumption
- ❌ Don't buy fake "Terracotta Warrior souvenirs"
