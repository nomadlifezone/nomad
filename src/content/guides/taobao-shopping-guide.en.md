# Complete Taobao Shopping Guide for Beginners

Taobao is China's largest online shopping platform with a rich variety of products at affordable prices. This guide teaches you how to use Taobao from scratch.

## 📱 Download and Registration

### Download App

**Taobao (Taobao)**:
- iOS: Search "Taobao" in App Store
- Android: Search "Taobao" in app stores

**International Version (Optional)**:
- **Taobao Lite**: Lightweight version, supports English
- **AliExpress**: International version (for overseas, higher prices)

### Register Account

**Method 1: Phone Number Registration** ⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐ (Recommended)
1. Open Taobao App
2. My Taobao → Login/Register
3. Select "Quick Registration"
4. Enter phone number (supports international numbers)
5. Get verification code
6. Set password
7. Complete registration

**Method 2: Alipay Login**
- Use existing Alipay account to login directly
- Auto-links

**Method 3: WeChat/QQ Login**
- Quick authorization login

### Complete Personal Information

**Required**:
- Nickname
- Avatar (optional)
- **Delivery Address** (Important!)

**Add Delivery Address**:
1. My Taobao → Settings → Address Management
2. Add new address
3. Fill in: Recipient name, phone number, detailed address (Province/City/District + Street + Door number), postal code (optional)
4. Set as default address

## 🛍️ How to Shop

### Step 1: Search for Products

**Search Methods**:
1. **Text Search**: Enter keywords in homepage search bar (e.g., "iPhone," "dress")

2. **Image Search** ⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐:
   - Tap camera icon in search bar
   - Upload image or take photo
   - Taobao identifies image to find similar items

3. **Browse Categories**: Tap categories on homepage, browse by category

4. **Barcode Scan**: Scan product barcode

### Step 2: Filter Products

**Filter Conditions**:
- Price range
- Sort by sales
- Comprehensive sort
- Credit sort
- Shipping location
- Whether Tmall (quality assurance)

**Check Products**:
1. **Images**: View from multiple angles
2. **Price**: Compare prices
3. **Sales**: High sales = Popular
4. **Reviews**:
   - Positive rate
   - Check buyer photos (real shots)
   - Check follow-up reviews
5. **Shop Info**:
   - Shop rating
   - Opening time
   - Credibility level

### Step 3: Shopping Tips

**Check Shop Type**:
- **Tmall Store** (⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐):
  - Red icon
  - Quality assurance
  - Genuine products
  - 7-day no-reason returns
  - Slightly higher prices

- **Regular Taobao Store** (⭐⭐⭐⭐):
  - Lower prices
  - More choices
  - Need careful identification

**Check Credibility**:
- 🔷 Diamond: 1-5 diamonds
- 👑 Crown: 1-5 crowns
- 🏆 Gold Crown: 1-5 gold crowns

**Focus on Reviews**:
- ✅ Read negative and neutral reviews (more realistic)
- ✅ Check follow-up reviews (long-term usage experience)
- ✅ See buyer photos (real images)
- ❌ Don't only read positive reviews (may be fake)

**Contact Customer Service**:
- Tap "Contact Customer Service"
- Ask details clearly: Size, material, shipping time, whether free shipping, return/exchange support

### Step 4: Place Order

**Add to Cart vs Buy Now**:
- **Add to Cart**: Continue shopping, combine orders, compare shops, wait for promotions

- **Buy Now**: Checkout directly, suitable for urgent needs

**Submit Order**:
1. Select product specifications (color, size, etc.)
2. Select quantity
3. Confirm delivery address
4. Choose delivery method (express/logistics)
5. Check discounts: Shop coupons, full reduction activities, red envelopes
6. Fill in notes (optional, e.g., "random color")
7. Confirm order amount
8. Tap "Submit Order"

### Step 5: Payment

**Payment Methods**:
1. **Alipay** (⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐ Recommended): Balance, linked bank card, Huabei (buy now pay later)

2. **WeChat Pay**

3. **Bank Card**

4. **Cash on Delivery** (some support)

**Payment Process**:
1. After submitting order, jump to payment page
2. Select payment method
3. Enter payment password
4. Payment successful

**Safety Tips**:
- ⚠️ Taobao has escrow transaction, payment held by platform first
- ⚠️ Money transferred to seller only after confirming receipt
- ⚠️ Don't pay offline
- ⚠️ Don't trust requests to add WeChat for transfers

## 📦 Logistics and Receipt

### Check Logistics

**Query Method**:
1. My Taobao → Awaiting Receipt
2. Tap order
3. View logistics info
4. Real-time track package

**Logistics Companies**:
- SF Express (fastest but expensive)
- ZTO, YTO, STO, Yunda (regular)
- China Post (remote areas)
- JD Logistics

**Delivery Time**:
- Same city: 1-2 days
- Same province: 2-3 days
- Cross-province: 3-7 days
- Remote areas: 7-15 days

### Receive Goods

**Receipt Methods**:

**1. Delivery to Door**:
- Courier calls or knocks
- Sign for package
- Can inspect before signing

**2. Station/Locker** (Common):
- Package placed at community station/locker
- Receive pickup code SMS
- Use code to collect package
- Suitable when not home

**3. Self-Pickup Point**: Cainiao Station, convenience store pickup

**Inspection**:
- ✅ Check packaging intact
- ✅ Verify item quantity
- ✅ Check items undamaged
- ❌ If problem, refuse or take photos as evidence

### Confirm Receipt

**When to Confirm**:
- After receiving and checking goods
- System will auto-remind

**How to Confirm**:
1. Open order
2. Tap "Confirm Receipt"
3. Payment transferred to seller

**Note**:
- ⚠️ Don't confirm receipt early
- ⚠️ Auto-confirms after 10-15 days
- ⚠️ Can only review after confirming

## 💬 Review and Share

### Review Order

**Review Entry**: My Taobao → Awaiting Review

**Review Content**:
- Product quality (1-5 stars)
- Seller service (1-5 stars)
- Logistics speed (1-5 stars)
- Description match (1-5 stars)

**Text Review**: Real experience, product pros/cons, usage experience

**Upload Photos**: Upload real photos (buyer show) to help other buyers

**Follow-up Review**: Can add follow-up within 15 days after receipt, long-term usage feedback

## 🔄 Returns and Exchanges

### Return/Exchange Process

**Initiate Refund/Return**:
1. Open order
2. Tap "Refund/Return"
3. Select reason: Don't want it, quality issue, size mismatch, received fake, description doesn't match
4. Upload photos (if problem)
5. Fill in description
6. Submit application

**Seller Handles**:
- Agrees: Process refund/return
- Refuses: Can appeal or Taobao intervenes

**Return Process** (if return needed):
1. After seller agrees
2. Send back items (buyer pays shipping or seller provides return shipping)
3. Fill in tracking number
4. Seller receives goods then refunds

**Refund Only** (No return):
- Suitable when item has problem but don't want to return
- Negotiate partial refund

### Taobao Intervention

**When to Apply**:
- Negotiation with seller fails
- Seller refuses reasonable refund
- Received fake goods

**How to Apply**:
1. Order details → "Request customer service intervention"
2. Upload evidence
3. Wait for Taobao to handle

**Decision**: Taobao decides based on evidence, usually favors buyer

## 🎯 Shopping Tips

### 1. Compare Prices

**Tools**: Price history query (browser plugins), price comparison apps, compare different platforms (JD, Pinduoduo)

**Tips**: Before mega sales, prices may increase then decrease. Watch price trends.

### 2. Avoid Pitfalls

**Identify Bad Reviews**:
- ❌ Image doesn't match description
- ❌ Many quality complaints
- ❌ Poor customer service attitude

**Choose Shops**:
- ✅ Prefer Tmall
- ✅ Check shop rating (4.8+)
- ✅ Check transaction volume

**Prevent Fakes**:
- ✅ Choose official flagship stores
- ✅ Be wary of too-low prices
- ✅ Check brand authorization

### 3. Money-Saving Tips

**Combine Orders**: Reach full reduction threshold, order with friends

**Collect Coupons**: Collect coupons before entering shop, follow shop for coupons

**Wait for Mega Sales**: Add non-urgent items to cart and wait for big sales

**Taobao Gold Coins**: Daily sign-in to accumulate

### 4. Communication Tips

**With Customer Service**:
- Ask details clearly
- Save chat records (as evidence)
- Polite but insist on rights

**Common Phrases**:
- "Is this item in stock?"
- "When will it ship?"
- "Do you support 7-day no-reason returns?"
- "Size recommendation?"
- "Do you have coupons?"

## 🆘 Common Questions

**Q: Can foreigners use Taobao?**
A: Yes! Supports international phone registration, international credit card payment (through Alipay), but only delivers to mainland China.

**Q: How to judge product quality?**
A: Check sales (high sales usually stable quality), reviews (especially negative), buyer photos, choose Tmall for more assurance.

**Q: Received item not satisfactory, what to do?**
A: 7-day no-reason return (Tmall), apply for refund/return, negotiate with seller, can request Taobao intervention if fails.

**Q: How to avoid fake goods?**
A: Choose official flagship stores, be wary of too-low prices, check authorization proof, verify authenticity after receipt.

**Q: Can Taobao use English?**
A: App mainly Chinese, can download Taobao Lite (has English), or use translation tools.

**Q: How long does delivery take?**
A: Generally 3-7 days, same city 1-2 days, remote areas may be longer.
