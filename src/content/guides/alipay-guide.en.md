# Complete Guide to Using Alipay for Foreigners

Alipay is one of China's dominant payment platforms, accepted by almost all merchants. This guide helps foreigners register and use Alipay smoothly in China.

## 📱 Registering for Alipay

### Step 1: Download the App
Download the Alipay app from your app store:
- iOS: Search "Alipay" on the App Store
- Android: Search "Alipay" on Google Play or your app store

Alipay supports English interface. You can select your language when you first open the app.

### Step 2: Register with Your Foreign Phone Number
1. Open the Alipay app
2. Tap "Register" or "Sign Up"
3. Select your country/region code (e.g., +1 for USA, +44 for UK)
4. Enter your phone number
5. Receive and enter the SMS verification code
6. Set a 6-digit login password
7. Complete registration

**Note**: Make sure your phone can receive international SMS messages.

### Step 3: Link Your International Bank Card
Alipay now supports international credit and debit cards:
- ✅ Visa
- ✅ Mastercard
- ✅ American Express
- ✅ JCB
- ✅ Diners Club

**Card Linking Steps**:
1. Go to "Me" → "Bank Cards"
2. Tap "Add Bank Card" or "Add Card"
3. Select "International Card"
4. Enter card number, expiration date, and CVV
5. Fill in cardholder name (must match the card)
6. Verify (may require SMS code or bank verification)
7. Card linked successfully

## 💳 How to Pay

### Scan to Pay (Most Common)
1. Open Alipay and tap "Scan" on the homepage
2. Scan the merchant's QR code
3. Enter the payment amount (if not preset by merchant)
4. Confirm the amount
5. Enter payment password or use Face ID/Touch ID
6. Payment complete

### Show Payment Code
1. Open Alipay and tap "Pay" on the homepage
2. Show your payment QR code/barcode for the merchant to scan
3. Enter payment password to confirm
4. Payment complete

### Online Payment
When shopping online, select Alipay as payment method. You'll be redirected to the Alipay app to complete payment.

## 💰 Top-up and Withdrawal

### Topping Up Balance
Due to international card restrictions, foreigners usually cannot directly top up their Alipay balance. However, you can:
- Pay directly with your linked international card (no top-up needed)
- Receive transfers from friends via the "Transfer" function

### Transfer Function
- You can receive transfers from other Alipay users
- Cannot directly withdraw to international bank cards
- Can use balance for shopping or payments

## ⚠️ Limitations and Notes

### Usage Restrictions
- **Daily Limits**: International credit cards typically have daily transaction limits (around ¥5,000-20,000)
- **Limited Features**: Some features require Chinese ID verification (e.g., investments, loans)
- **Merchant Restrictions**: Very few merchants only accept WeChat Pay

### Fees
- **Payments**: Using international cards usually has no service fee
- **Currency Conversion**: Banks may charge foreign exchange fees (3-5%)
- **International Transaction Fee**: Some banks charge international transaction fees

### Security Tips
- ✅ Set a complex payment password
- ✅ Enable fingerprint or Face ID
- ✅ Never share your account password with others
- ✅ Link an email for password recovery
- ✅ Regularly check transaction history

## 🔧 Common Questions

### Q1: Unable to link international credit card?
**A**: Try these solutions:
1. Confirm your card supports international transactions
2. Try a different card
3. Contact your bank to enable international online payments
4. Use alternative payment methods (WeChat Pay)

### Q2: Payment shows insufficient balance?
**A**: International card users don't need to top up. Make sure:
1. You're paying with "International Bank Card" not "Balance"
2. Your linked card has sufficient funds
3. You haven't exceeded daily transaction limits

### Q3: Can I use Alipay to call a taxi?
**A**: Yes! Alipay can:
- Call taxis via Amap
- Order food delivery on Ele.me
- Shop on Taobao
- Top up mobile phone credit
- Buy movie tickets

### Q4: Can I still use Alipay after leaving China?
**A**: Yes, but with limitations:
- Can use at overseas merchants that accept Alipay
- Can transfer to other users
- Some features require Chinese phone number for SMS verification

### Q5: Alipay or WeChat Pay - which is better?
**A**: Each has advantages:
- **Alipay**: More features, better international card support
- **WeChat Pay**: More widely used, strong social features
- **Recommendation**: Download both for different scenarios
