# Complete Guide to Food Delivery in China

Food delivery has become an integral part of daily life in China. This guide teaches you how to use Meituan Waimai and Ele.me to easily enjoy delivery services.

## 📱 Main Delivery Platforms

### Meituan vs Ele.me

| Platform | Market Share | Features | Rating |
|----------|--------------|----------|---------|
| Meituan Waimai | 65% | Most merchants, fast delivery | ⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐ |
| Ele.me | 30% | Alibaba-owned, many discounts | ⭐⭐⭐⭐ |

**Recommendation**: Download both, compare prices and discounts before ordering

## 🔐 Registration and Setup

**Download Apps**:
- Meituan Waimai: Search in App Store
- Ele.me: Search or use through Alipay mini-program

**Registration**: Phone number, WeChat, or Apple ID

**Key: Add Delivery Address** ⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐
- Be detailed: Building, unit, room number
- Add contact name and phone
- Can save multiple addresses

## 🍱 How to Order

### 6-Step Process

1. **Select Restaurant**: Browse, search, or filter
2. **Add Items**: Choose dishes, quantity, specifications
3. **Use Discounts**: Coupons, full reduction, red envelopes
4. **Submit Order**: Confirm address, payment, notes
5. **Track Delivery**: Real-time rider location
6. **Receive Food**: Check items, confirm receipt

### Key Features

- **Schedule Orders**: Order breakfast/lunch in advance
- **Group Orders**: Order with colleagues/roommates
- **Contact Rider**: Call or message through app
- **Order History**: Reorder favorite meals

## 💰 Cost Breakdown

| Item | Amount | Notes |
|------|--------|-------|
| Food Price | By dish | Merchant pricing |
| Packaging Fee | ¥1-3 | Box fee |
| Delivery Fee | ¥2-8 | By distance |
| Discounts | -¥X | Coupons, etc. |
| **Total** | **Final** | What you pay |

### Membership

**Meituan/Ele.me VIP**: ¥15-25/month
- Benefits: Monthly coupons, free delivery, priority delivery

## 🎯 Tips and Notes

### Money-Saving Tips
1. Collect coupons daily
2. Order during off-peak hours
3. Group orders to reach discount thresholds
4. Compare platforms

### Safety and Etiquette
- Check food packaging integrity
- Note allergies in remarks
- Be understanding during bad weather
- Rate riders fairly

### For Non-Chinese Speakers
- Order by looking at photos
- Use translation app for menu
- Choose chain restaurants (may have English)
- Ask hotel staff for help

## 🆘 Common Issues

**Q: Can foreigners use food delivery apps?**
A: Yes! Supports international phone numbers and payment through Alipay/WeChat (with international cards).

**Q: How to fill address?**
A: Use location or search hotel name. Fill in detailed room number. Can use translation app for Chinese address.

**Q: Delivery too slow?**
A: Peak hours (11:30-13:00, 18:00-19:30) are slower. Can contact rider or request refund.

**Q: Problem with food?**
A: Take photos, apply for refund through order details.

## 📝 Quick Start

**Preparation**:
- ✅ Download Meituan/Ele.me
- ✅ Register and add detailed delivery address
- ✅ Bind payment method
- ✅ Claim new user coupons

**First Order**:
1. Select restaurant (check ratings and reviews)
2. Add items to cart
3. Use available coupons
4. Confirm address and notes
