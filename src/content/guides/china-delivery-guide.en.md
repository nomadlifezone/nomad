### SF Express (顺丰速运) ⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐

**Recommended for important packages**

**Features**:
- Fastest delivery in China (same day to 2 days)
- Reliable tracking
- Good customer service
- Temperature-controlled options
- Door-to-door service

**Price Range**:
- Within city: ¥12-15
- Within province: ¥13-18
- Cross-province: ¥18-30
- Coastal to inland: ¥25-40

### YTO (圆通速递)

**Popular budget option**

**Features**:
- Good coverage
- Affordable prices
- Acceptable delivery times
- Weaker tracking system

**Price Range**:
- 30-50% cheaper than SF

### EMS (中国邮政速递)

**Best for international shipping**

**Features**:
- Government-backed reliability
- Extensive global network
- Handles customs better
- Slower than private couriers

## 📦 How to Send a Package

### Domestic Shipping Process

**Step 1: Prepare Your Package**

1. **Packaging**:
   - Use appropriate box or envelope
   - Fragile items: use bubble wrap
   - Liquids: double-seal, separate packaging

2. **Size and Weight**:
   - Most companies limit to 30-50kg
   - Oversized packages cost more
   - Measure actual size (L × W × H)

**Step 2: Choose a Service**

| Service | Speed | Price | Use Case |
|---------|-------|-------|----------|
| Standard | 2-3 days | Budget | Non-urgent |
| Express | Next day | Medium | Important |
| Same-day | 2-6 hours | Premium | Urgent |

**Step 3: Go to Service Point or Schedule Pickup**

**Option A: Drop off at store**
- Find nearest franchise store (便利店 often has)
- Look for logos: 顺丰, 圆通, etc.
- Staff will help

**Option B: Schedule pickup**
- Use app (preferred for SF, JD)
- Call hotline
- Some services free, some ¥5-10

**Step 4: Fill in Information**

1. **Sender info**: Name, phone, address
2. **Receiver info**: Name, phone, full address
3. **Package contents**: For customs (if international)
4. **Declared value**: For insurance

**Step 5: Pay and Get Tracking Number**

- Pay by cash, Alipay, or WeChat
- Save tracking number (运单号)
- Get receipt

## 📍 Addressing in China

### Address Format

**Chinese Format**:
```
[Province] [City] [District] [Street] [Building] [Unit]
Example:
广东省 广州市 天河区 天河路 123号 10楼 1001室
```

**English Format**:
```
[Unit] [Building] [Street] [District] [City] [Province] [Postal Code]
Example:
Room 1001, Building 123, Tianhe Road, Tianhe District, Guangzhou, Guangdong 510000
```

### Tips for Writing Addresses

- Include postal code (邮编)
- Write phone number clearly
- Add landmarks if possible
- For English delivery, print both Chinese and English

## 💰 Pricing Examples

### Domestic Shipping (SF Express)

| Route | Weight | Price | Time |
|-------|--------|-------|------|
| City to same city | 1kg | ¥12-15 | Same day |
| Guangzhou to Shenzhen | 1kg | ¥13-18 | Next day |
| Guangzhou to Beijing | 1kg | ¥23-30 | 1-2 days |
| Guangzhou to Shanghai | 1kg | ¥18-25 | 1-2 days |

### Price Factors

- **Distance**: Longer distance = more expensive
- **Weight**: Heavier = more expensive (actual weight)
- **Dimensions**: Large but light packages charged by volumetric weight
- **Service type**: Express/same-day costs more

## 🌍 International Shipping from China

### Major International Options

| Service | Speed | Coverage | Best For |
|---------|-------|----------|----------|
| **EMS** | 7-15 days | Global | Customs handling ⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐ |
| **DHL** | 3-7 days | Global | Fast, reliable ⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐ |
| **FedEx** | 3-7 days | Global | Documents ⭐⭐⭐⭐ |
| **UPS** | 3-7 days | Global | Business ⭐⭐⭐⭐ |
| **SF International** | 5-10 days | Limited | Asia ⭐⭐⭐⭐ |

### EMS International

**Best for most people**

**Advantages**:
- Handles customs documentation
- Global coverage
- More affordable than DHL/FedEx
- Tracks to destination

**Price Examples** (to USA):
- 0.5kg: ¥150-180
- 1kg: ¥220-260
- 2kg: ¥340-400

### DHL/FedEx

**For urgent/business shipments**

**Advantages**:
- Fastest (3-7 days)
- Excellent tracking
- Reliable

**Price Examples** (to USA):
- 0.5kg: ¥250-300
- 1kg: ¥350-450

### International Shipping Process

1. **Prepare items**:
   - Check import restrictions of destination country
   - Remove batteries if required
   - Proper packaging

2. **Customs documentation**:
   - Commercial invoice (if commercial)
   - Contents list
   - Value declaration
   - ID copy (passport)

3. **Choose carrier**:
   - EMS for customs ease
   - DHL/FedEx for speed

4. **Take to carrier or schedule pickup**

5. **Pay shipping and any duties**

## 📦 Packing Tips

### Standard Packing

**Box Selection**:
- Right size for item
- Sturdy cardboard
- No extra empty space

**Packing Materials**:
- Bubble wrap for electronics
- Packing peanuts for fragile
- Tape all openings

**Labeling**:
- Clear sender and receiver info
- "Fragile" stickers if needed
- "This Side Up" arrows

### What Cannot Be Shipped

- ❌ Lithium batteries (some services)
- ❌ Flammable items
- ❌ Cash and valuables
- ❌ Controlled substances
- ❌ Fresh food (some restrictions)
- ❌ Counterfeit goods

## 📋 Common Documents Needed

### For Domestic Shipping

- **ID** (sometimes required)

### For International Shipping

- **Passport copy**
- **Customs declaration** (itemized)
- **Commercial invoice** (if commercial)
- **Permits** (if applicable)

## 💡 Pro Tips

### Save Money

1. **Compare carriers** - prices vary significantly
2. **Use Cainiao** for comparison
3. **Ship during off-peak** (cheaper)
4. **Proper weight declaration** (avoid surprises)

### Avoid Problems

1. **Double-check address** - Chinese addresses are detailed
2. **Include phone number** - delivery person needs to contact you
3. **Be home for large packages**
4. **Use SF for valuable items**

### For Foreigners

1. **Use apps with English** (SF, Cainiao)
2. **Write address in Chinese** (for local delivery)
3. **Keep passport ready** for international shipping
4. **Use English packing list** for customs
