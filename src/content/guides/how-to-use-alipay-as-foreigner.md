# How to Use Alipay as a Foreigner in China

Alipay is the dominant payment platform in China. Here's everything you need to know to use it as a foreigner.

## Setting Up Alipay

### Step 1: Download the App
Download Alipay from your app store. The app supports English interface.

### Step 2: Register with Foreign Phone Number
You can register using your international phone number (+1, +44, etc.). Make sure you have access to receive SMS verification codes.

### Step 3: Add Your International Bank Card
Alipay now supports international cards from major networks (Visa, Mastercard, American Express). Look for the "International cards" option in the payment settings.

## Making Payments

1. Open Alipay and tap the "Pay" button
2. Scan the merchant's QR code
3. Confirm the amount
4. Enter your password or use Face ID

## Limitations

- Some merchants only accept WeChat Pay
- Daily transaction limits apply to international cards
- Some features require Chinese ID verification

## Tips

- Always keep a backup payment method (cash)
- Download offline maps in case payment apps fail
- Learn to say "Alipay" (支付宝) — most merchants will understand
