# Complete Guide to China High-Speed Rail

China's high-speed rail is the world's most advanced railway system, with over 40,000 km of operational tracks. This guide teaches you how to book tickets, board trains, and enjoy the convenience of China's high-speed rail.

## 🎫 How to Buy Tickets

### 12306 Official App (Recommended) ⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐
China Railway's official booking platform, now supports foreign passport registration.

**Registration Process**:
1. Download 12306 App
2. Select "Foreign Passport" registration
3. Enter passport information
4. Face recognition verification
5. Complete registration

**Booking Process**:
1. Select departure and destination
2. Choose date and train number
3. Add passenger information
4. Select seat class
5. Online payment (Alipay/WeChat/International cards)

### Third-Party Platforms: Ctrip/Fliggy ⭐⭐⭐⭐
- English-friendly interface
- Small service fee (¥5-10)
- Quick customer service response

## 🚄 Seat Classes

| Class | Price | Features | Best For |
|-------|-------|----------|---------|
| Business | 1.8x First Class | Lie-flat, spacious | Business travel |
| First Class | 1.6x Second Class | 2+2 layout, comfortable | Long journeys |
| Second Class | Base price | 3+2 layout, best value | Most travelers |

**Price Examples**:
- Beijing-Shanghai: ¥553 (2nd) / ¥933 (1st) - 4.5 hours
- Shanghai-Guangzhou: ¥793 (2nd) / ¥1,268 (1st) - 7 hours

## 🚉 Boarding Process

### Before Departure
- Arrive 30-60 minutes early
- Prepare passport/ID
- Confirm train number and seat

### Entry Steps
1. **Security Check** - Luggage through X-ray
2. **ID Verification** - Scan passport + face recognition
3. **Waiting Area** - Check display board for gate info
4. **Ticket Checking** - Starts 10-15 minutes before departure
5. **Board Train** - Find your seat by number

### Onboard Services
- Dining car with boxed meals (¥30-45)
- Power outlets at each seat
- Hot water and restrooms in each carriage
- WiFi available on some trains

## 🔄 Ticket Changes & Refunds

### Change Rules
- Free changes once before departure
- Can only change to same-day trains
- Changes via App available 2+ hours before departure

### Refund Rules
- 15+ days before: No fee
- 15 days-48 hours: 5% fee
- 48-24 hours: 10% fee
- Within 24 hours: 20% fee

## 💰 Money-Saving Tips

1. **Book Early** - Purchase 15-30 days in advance
2. **Off-Peak Times** - Early morning/late night trains cheaper
3. **Points Redemption** - 12306 points can offset tickets
4. **Platform Discounts** - Ctrip/Fliggy often have coupons

## ⚠️ Important Notes

### Things to Know
- Foreigners must carry original passport
- Most stations support e-tickets, no need to collect physical tickets
- Prohibited items: knives, lighters, flammable materials
- Ticket checking stops 5 minutes before departure

### Useful Information
- Station WiFi: "ChinaRailway-WiFi"
- 12306 Hotline: 95105105
- Waiting areas have charging zones
- Large luggage racks at carriage ends

## 🎯 Quick Start Checklist

- ✅ Download and register on 12306 App
- ✅ Link payment method
- ✅ Add frequent passengers
- ✅ Understand seat classes and prices
- ✅ Learn change and refund rules
- ✅ Plan and book tickets in advance

Enjoy your China high-speed rail journey! 🚄✨
