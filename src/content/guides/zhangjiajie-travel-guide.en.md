# Complete Guide to Zhangjiajie National Forest Park

Zhangjiajie gained worldwide fame as the filming location for Avatar. With over 3,000 unique rock pillars, it's one of China's most spectacular natural wonders.

## 🗺️ Overview

Core scenic areas include:
- **Zhangjiajie National Forest Park** (Wulingyuan) ⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐
- **Tianmen Mountain** (City center) ⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐
- **Grand Canyon Glass Bridge** (Cili County) ⭐⭐⭐⭐
- **Yellow Dragon Cave** (Wulingyuan) ⭐⭐⭐

## 🎫 Ticket Information

### Zhangjiajie National Forest Park
- **Ticket**: ¥228 (Valid for 4 days)
- **Includes**: Forest Park, Golden Whip Stream, Yuanjiajie, Yangjiajie, Tianzi Mountain
- **Cable Cars** (Separate charges):
  - Bailong Elevator: ¥72 (one-way)
  - Tianzi Mountain Cable Car: ¥72 (one-way)
  - Huangshi Village Cable Car: ¥118 (round trip)

### Tianmen Mountain
- **Ticket**: ¥258 (includes cable car)
- **Glass Walkway**: ¥5 (shoe covers)

### Grand Canyon Glass Bridge
- **Ticket**: ¥219 (includes bridge)
- **Advance booking required**

## 📅 Best Time to Visit

### Recommended Seasons
- **Spring (Mar-May)**: Blooming flowers, misty mountains ⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐
- **Autumn (Sep-Nov)**: Clear skies, autumn foliage ⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐
- **Summer (Jun-Aug)**: Cool but rainy, watch for slippery paths ⭐⭐⭐
- **Winter (Dec-Feb)**: Possible closures, spectacular snow scenery ⭐⭐⭐⭐

### Avoid Peak Times
- Skip National Day and Spring Festival holidays
- Weekdays have fewer crowds

## 🚶 Recommended Itineraries

### 3-Day Classic Route

**Day 1: East Route**
- Morning: Golden Whip Stream hike (3 hours)
- Noon: Lunch at Shuiraosimen
- Afternoon: Bailong Elevator to Yuanjiajie
- Evening: Stay on mountain top

**Day 2: West Route**
- Morning: Yuanjiajie (Avatar Hallelujah Mountain)
- Noon: Lunch on mountain
- Afternoon: Yangjiajie (Tianbo Mansion, Wulongzhai)
- Evening: Continue mountain stay

**Day 3: Tianzi Mountain + Descent**
- Morning: Tianzi Mountain (Imperial Brush Peaks)
- Noon: Mountain lunch
- Afternoon: Cable car down or Ten-Mile Gallery hike
- Evening: Return to city

### 4-Day In-Depth Route
Add to 3-day itinerary:
- **Day 4**: Tianmen Mountain (Glass Walkway, Heaven's Gate)

## 🏨 Accommodation

### Mountain Top ⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐ (Recommended)
**Pros**:
- Save travel time
- Watch sunrise/sunset
- Deep exploration

**Recommended Areas**:
- Dingxiangrong Village (central location)
- Daguantai Village (sunrise views)
- Yuanjiajie (near Avatar sites)

**Price**: ¥100-300/night (standard room)

### Wulingyuan Town ⭐⭐⭐⭐
- Near park entrance
- Many options, affordable
- Price: ¥80-200/night

### Zhangjiajie City ⭐⭐⭐
- Convenient transportation
- 40-minute drive to park
- Price: ¥100-300/night

## 🚗 Transportation

### Getting to Zhangjiajie
**Flight**: Zhangjiajie Hehua Airport
- Airport to city: Taxi ¥30-40, 30 minutes

**High-Speed Rail**: Zhangjiajie West Station
- Station to city: Taxi ¥20-30, 20 minutes

### Scenic Area Transportation
- City to Forest Park: Bus ¥12, 40 minutes
- City to Tianmen Mountain: Taxi ¥15-20, 15 minutes
- Free shuttle buses within park

## ⚠️ Important Notes

### 📋 Essential Items
- Hiking shoes (non-slip)
- Raincoat/umbrella
- Sunscreen
- Power bank
- Cash (some mountain shops)

### 🏔️ Safety Tips
1. Steep paths, watch your step
2. Slow down in rain
3. Don't climb railings for photos
4. Secure valuables
5. Monitor weather changes

### 💡 Travel Tips
1. Book mountain accommodation 1-2 days ahead
2. Enter park before 8am to avoid crowds
3. Pack light
4. Download park map app
5. Wear comfortable hiking shoes

## 🎯 Money-Saving Tips

1. **Ticket Discounts**
   - Half price for students
   - Half price for 60+, free for 70+

2. **Accommodation**
   - Book in advance for discounts
   - Lower prices in low season
   - Consider youth hostels

3. **Food**
   - Bring snacks
   - Avoid scenic area restaurants
   - Mountain prices 30-50% higher

4. **Cable Cars**
   - Hike if fit
   - Use only essential cable cars
