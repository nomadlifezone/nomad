# Complete Guilin Li River Cruise Guide

"Guilin's scenery is the best under heaven, Yangshuo's scenery is the best in Guilin." The Li River cruise is the best way to appreciate Guilin's landscape. This guide helps you explore the Li River highlights.

## 🚢 Route Comparison

### Route 1: Guilin-Yangshuo Full Cruise (Recommended) ⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐

**Route**: Zhujiang Pier (Guilin) → Yangshuo (83 km)
**Duration**: About 4-4.5 hours
**Price**: 
- Regular boat: ¥215 (online booking)
- Luxury boat: ¥450-550
- AC boat: ¥360

**Includes**: Ticket, Chinese lunch (simple meal), guide commentary

**Pros**:
- ✅ See all Li River highlights
- ✅ The scenery from 20 RMB note
- ✅ One-way trip, no backtracking

**Best For**: First-time visitors wanting complete Li River experience

### Route 2: Yangdi-Xingping Highlight Tour ⭐⭐⭐⭐

**Route**: Yangdi Pier → Xingping Ancient Town (25 km)
**Method**: Bamboo raft drifting
**Duration**: 2-3 hours
**Price**: ¥95-120/person (4 people per raft)

**Pros**:
- ✅ Affordable
- ✅ Covers main highlights
- ✅ Includes 20 RMB note scenery
- ✅ Bamboo raft closer to water

**Best For**: Budget travelers seeking value

### Route 3: City Cruise ⭐⭐⭐

**Route**: Guilin city Two Rivers Four Lakes
**Duration**: 1-2 hours
**Price**: ¥100-200
**Features**: Night cruise, city views

## 🗺️ Scenic Spots Along the Way

### Main Attractions (in order)

**1. Yellow Cloth Reflection** ⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐
- The scene from 20 RMB note
- Best photo spot
- Crystal clear water like mirror

**2. Nine Horse Mural Hill** ⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐
- Mountain wall resembles nine horses
- Legend says smart people can see all 9 horses
- Guide will explain

**3. Ox Gorge**
- River narrows
- Cliffs on both sides

**4. Xingping Ancient Town**
- 1,700-year history
- Bamboo raft tour endpoint

### Photo Tips
- 📷 **Best Time**: Morning 9-10 AM, afternoon 3-4 PM (soft light)
- 📷 **20 RMB Background**: Prepare 20 RMB note for comparison photo
- 📷 **Yellow Cloth**: Most beautiful on sunny days when water is calm
- 📷 **Equipment**: Phone is fine, bring power bank

## 🚗 Transportation Guide

### Getting to Guilin
**Flight**: Guilin Liangjiang International Airport
- Airport bus to city: ¥20, 40 minutes
- Taxi: ¥80-100

**High-speed Rail**: Guilin North/Guilin Station
- Metro/bus to city

### Pier Transportation

**Zhujiang Pier** (full cruise start):
- 30 km from city
- Travel agencies provide pick-up
- Taxi about ¥50-80
- Bus: Special line from bus station

### Yangshuo to Guilin Return
- **Bus**: Yangshuo Bus Station, ¥20, 1.5 hours
- **High-speed Rail**: Yangshuo Station, to Guilin 30 minutes
- **Chartered Car**: ¥150-200

## ⚠️ Important Notes

### Best Travel Time
- **Spring (Mar-May)**: Flowers blooming, misty scenery ⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐
- **Autumn (Sep-Nov)**: Clear weather, moderate water ⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐
- **Summer (Jun-Aug)**: Rainy season, may suspend ⭐⭐⭐
- **Winter (Dec-Feb)**: Less water but clear, cold ⭐⭐⭐

### Practical Tips
1. Check weather in advance (rainy days less scenic)
2. Bring sunscreen and hat
3. Strong AC on boat, bring light jacket
4. Motion sickness: bring medication
5. Bring power bank (photography drains battery)
6. Travel light (luggage storage inconvenient)

### Cautions
- ⚠️ Rainy season (May-Jun) may suspend service, confirm in advance
- ⚠️ Don't trust street touts or "black guides"
- ⚠️ Buy tickets from official channels
- ⚠️ Follow safety instructions on boat
- ⚠️ Don't feed fish from boat

- Duration: 2 hours
