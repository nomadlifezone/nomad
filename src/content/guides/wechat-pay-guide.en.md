# Complete WeChat Pay Guide for Foreigners

WeChat Pay is one of China's most commonly used payment methods, integrated into the WeChat App. This guide teaches you how to register WeChat and use WeChat Pay.

## 📱 Download and Register WeChat

**Download WeChat**:
- iOS: Search "WeChat" in App Store
- Android: Search "WeChat" in Google Play

**Registration**:
1. Open WeChat
2. Tap "Sign Up"
3. Select country code
4. Enter phone number
5. Receive SMS code
6. Set nickname and password

## 💳 Activate WeChat Pay

### Bind International Bank Card ⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐ (Recommended)

**Supported Cards**:
- ✅ Visa, Mastercard, Amex, JCB, Diners Club

**Steps**:
1. Me → Services (or Payment)
2. Wallet → Bank Cards → Add Card
3. Enter card number, expiry, CVV
4. Enter cardholder name (must match card)
5. Verify and complete

## 💰 How to Use WeChat Pay

### 1. Scan to Pay (Most Common)

- Open WeChat → + → Scan
- Scan merchant QR code
- Enter amount (if needed)
- Confirm payment

### 2. Show Payment Code

- Me → Services → Money
- Show QR/barcode to merchant
- Automatic deduction

### 3. Transfer Money

- Open friend chat → + → Transfer
- Enter amount and confirm

### 4. Red Envelopes

**Send**: Chat → + → Red Envelope
**Receive**: Tap red envelope in chat

**Red Envelope Culture**:
- Sent during festivals/birthdays
- Lucky numbers: ¥6.66, ¥8.88, ¥66.66

## 💡 Useful Features

1. **Transit Code**: Pay for metro/bus
2. **Mobile Top-up**: Add credit to phone
3. **Bill Payment**: Utilities (requires real-name verification)
4. **Split Bill (AA)**: Share costs with friends
5. **WeChat Wallet**: Store balance from transfers/red envelopes

## 🔒 Security Settings

- Set payment password (6 digits)
- Enable fingerprint/face ID
- Set delayed arrival time (to prevent scams)
- Never share password

## 💰 Limits and Fees

### Limits
- Unverified: ¥1,000/day, ¥2,000/year
- Verified: ¥50,000+/transaction

### Fees
- ✅ Scanning payment: Free
- ✅ Transfers: Free
- ✅ Red envelopes: Free
- 💰 Withdraw to bank: 0.1% (¥1,000 lifetime free quota)
- 💰 International card currency conversion: Bank charges (3-5%)

## ⚠️ Limitations for Foreigners

- ❌ Cannot top up balance (with international card)
- ❌ Some wealth management features unavailable
- ❌ Some bill payment features restricted
- ✅ CAN scan to pay
- ✅ CAN transfer and receive money
- ✅ CAN send/receive red envelopes

## 🎯 Common Questions

**Q: Card binding failed?**
A: Ensure card supports international online transactions. Contact bank. Try another card.

**Q: Can use without real-name verification?**
A: Yes, but limited (¥1,000/day limit). Binding international card provides partial verification.

**Q: Any fees with international cards?**
A: WeChat doesn't charge fees, but banks charge currency conversion fees (3-5%).

**Q: WeChat Pay safe?**
A: Very safe with encryption and insurance protection.
