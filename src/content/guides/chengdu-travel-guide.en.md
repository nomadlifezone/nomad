# Complete Chengdu Travel Guide - Panda & Hotpot Capital

Chengdu, a city you won't want to leave once you arrive. Here you'll find adorable giant pandas, spicy hotpot, and leisurely tea house culture. This guide helps you experience the most authentic Chengdu slow life.

## 🗺️ Must-Visit Attractions

### 1. Chengdu Research Base of Giant Panda Breeding ⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐

**Admission**: ¥55 (advance online booking required)
**Hours**: 7:30-18:00 (arrive before 8 AM recommended)

**Best Visit Time**:
- Morning 7:30-9:00: Pandas most active, breakfast feeding show
- Afternoon: Pandas mostly sleeping, less engaging

**Must-See Areas**:
- Moon Nursery (baby pandas)
- Sun Nursery
- Sub-adult Panda Villa
- Adult Panda Area

**Duration**: 3-4 hours
**Transportation**: Metro Line 3 to Panda Avenue Station + bus/taxi

### 2. Kuanzhai Alley ⭐⭐⭐⭐

**Features**: Qing Dynasty ancient street, Chengdu cultural landmark

**Three Alleys**:
- **Wide Alley**: Old Chengdu life experience
- **Narrow Alley**: Slow life leisure area
- **Well Alley**: Folk culture display

**Admission**: Free
**Duration**: 2-3 hours
**Best Time**: Evening (more beautiful night view)

**Experiences**:
- Ear cleaning (¥30-50)
- Sipping bowl tea
- Sichuan opera face-changing performance
- Photo opportunities

### 3. Jinli Ancient Street ⭐⭐⭐⭐

**Features**: Three Kingdoms culture themed street (near Wuhou Temple)

**Food Recommendations**:
- Three Cannons (dessert)
- Sugar Oil Fruit
- Egg Pancake
- Bobo Chicken

**Admission**: Free
**Duration**: 2 hours

### 4. Wuhou Temple ⭐⭐⭐⭐

**Admission**: ¥50
**Features**: Three Kingdoms culture sanctuary
**Duration**: 1.5-2 hours

### 5. Du Fu Thatched Cottage ⭐⭐⭐⭐

**Admission**: ¥50
**Features**: Former residence of Tang poet Du Fu
**Duration**: 1.5-2 hours
**Environment**: Serene garden, perfect for leisurely stroll

### 6. Wenshu Monastery ⭐⭐⭐⭐

**Admission**: Free
**Features**: Zen Buddhist temple, vegetarian meals
**Duration**: 1 hour

**Recommendations**:
- Wenshu Monastery Zen Tea (¥30-50)
- Vegetarian buffet (¥50-80)

### 7. Chunxi Road/Taikoo Li ⭐⭐⭐⭐

**Features**: Chengdu's busiest commercial district

**Chunxi Road**: Budget-friendly shopping
**Taikoo Li**: High-end brands + internet celebrity check-in

**Must Check-In**:
- IFS Giant Panda Sculpture (rooftop)
- Fangsuo Bookstore
- Line Friends Flagship Store

### 8. People's Park ⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐

**Admission**: Free
**Features**: Experience Chengdu slow life

**Must Experience**:
- Heming Tea House tea (¥20-30/cup)
- Ear cleaning (¥30-50)
- Watch elderly playing mahjong
- Matchmaking corner (weekends)

**Duration**: 1-2 hours

## 🍜 Chengdu Cuisine

### Must-Try Food TOP 10

**1. Hotpot** ⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐
**Recommended Restaurants**:
- Shudaxia (internet celebrity, good value)
- Xiaolongkan (chain brand)
- Dalongyi (popular with young people)
- Chuanxibazi (locals frequent)

**Per Person**: ¥80-150
**Ordering Tips**:
- Pot: Mandarin duck pot (spicy + non-spicy)
- Must-order: Tripe, duck intestine, beef throat, brain

**2. Skewers** ⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐
**Recommended**:
- Maojiao Huola
- Gangguanchang Wuqu Xiaoyuejun Ganchuan

**Per Person**: ¥50-80
**Feature**: Bamboo skewered dishes, charged by stick count

**3. Bobo Chicken** ⭐⭐⭐⭐
**Recommended**: Yepopo Bobo Chicken
**Price**: ¥1-3/stick
**Feature**: Cold skewers, green pepper or red oil

**4. Maocai** ⭐⭐⭐⭐
**Recommended**: Maojiao Huola, Jixiang Huntun Maocai
**Per Person**: ¥25-40
**Feature**: Solo hotpot

**5. Husband and Wife Lung Slices** ⭐⭐⭐⭐
**Recommended**: Husband and Wife Lung Slices Main Store
**Price**: ¥35-50
**Feature**: Classic Sichuan cold dish

**6. Dan Dan Noodles** ⭐⭐⭐⭐
**Recommended**: Dongzikou Zhang Lao Er Liangfen
**Price**: ¥8-12
**Feature**: Spicy, salty, savory

**7. Sweet Water Noodles** ⭐⭐⭐⭐
**Recommended**: Dongzikou Zhang Lao Er Liangfen
**Price**: ¥8-10
**Feature**: Unique sweet-spicy flavor

**8. Dragon Wontons** ⭐⭐⭐⭐
**Recommended**: Long Chaoshou Main Store
**Price**: ¥20-30
**Feature**: Chengdu-style wontons

**9. Rabbit Head** ⭐⭐⭐⭐
**Recommended**: Shuangliu Laoma Rabbit Head
**Price**: ¥15-25/piece
**Feature**: Spicy fragrant, gnaw to eat

**10. Desserts**
- **Ice Jelly**: ¥8-12 (summer must-eat)
- **Three Cannons**: ¥10 (Jinli)
- **Egg Pancake**: ¥5-8

### Must-Visit Food Streets

**1. Jianshe Road Snack Street** ⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐
- Students' favorite
- Cheap prices
- Rich variety

**2. Kuixinglou Street** ⭐⭐⭐⭐

- Hotpot, skewers concentrated
- Suitable for young people

- Rich nightlife

- Tourist concentrated
- Prices higher
- Good for experience

## ☕ Chengdu Tea House Culture

### Recommended Tea Houses

1. **Heming Tea House** (People's Park)
   - Most authentic old Chengdu flavor
   - Price: ¥20-30/cup

2. **Daci Temple Zen Tea**
   - Temple tea house
   - Serene environment

3. **Kuanzhai Alley Tea Houses**
   - Tourist-oriented
   - Price: ¥40-80/cup

### Tea House Experience
- Sipping bowl tea
- Ear cleaning service
- Watch Sichuan opera face-changing
- Play mahjong (some tea houses)

## ⚠️ Important Notes

### Dining Cautions
- 🌶️ Non-spicy eaters must say "slightly spicy" or "mandarin duck pot"
- Pair hotpot with yogurt/soy milk to protect stomach
- Bring stomach medicine
- Drink plenty of water

### Best Travel Time
- **Spring (Mar-May)**: Comfortable climate ⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐
- **Autumn (Sep-Nov)**: Pleasant weather ⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐
- **Summer (Jun-Aug)**: Hot and rainy ⭐⭐⭐
- **Winter (Dec-Feb)**: Cold and damp ⭐⭐⭐

### Practical Tips
1. Must arrive early at panda base
2. Book popular hotpot restaurants in advance
3. Download Dianping to find food
4. Prepare stomach medicine
5. Kuanzhai Alley, Jinli more beautiful at night
