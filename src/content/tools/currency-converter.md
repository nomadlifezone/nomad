This currency converter helps you convert between major world currencies using live exchange rates from the Frankfurter API.

## Supported Currencies

- **USD** — US Dollar
- **EUR** — Euro
- **CNY** — Chinese Yuan
- **GBP** — British Pound
- **JPY** — Japanese Yen
- **AUD** — Australian Dollar
- **CAD** — Canadian Dollar

## How to Use

Enter an amount in any currency field, and the tool will automatically calculate conversions for all other currencies in real-time.

## Data Source

Exchange rates are provided by [Frankfurter API](https://www.frankfurter.app/), updated daily from the European Central Bank.

## Disclaimer

These rates are for informational purposes only. For exact conversions, please check with your bank or a financial institution.
